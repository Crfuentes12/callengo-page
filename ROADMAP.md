# Callengo Marketing Website Roadmap
## Q1-Q2 2026

> Strategic roadmap for the Callengo marketing website. Updated: March 13, 2026

---

## Mission

Drive qualified lead acquisition for Callengo's AI phone agent platform through a high-converting, SEO-optimized marketing website that clearly communicates value to our target markets: Healthcare, Real Estate, SaaS, Financial Services, and Legal.

---

## Strategic Priorities

### 1. **Conversion Optimization** (Critical)
**Owner:** CMO → Marketing Specialist
**Timeline:** Q1 2026

**Objectives:**
- Increase homepage → signup conversion by 25%
- Reduce bounce rate on product pages by 15%
- Improve demo interaction rate by 40%

**Key Initiatives:**
- A/B test hero messaging and CTAs
- Optimize demo player UX (faster load, clearer value prop)
- Add social proof (customer logos, live stats counter)
- Implement exit-intent popup with lead magnet
- Create industry-specific landing pages (Healthcare, Real Estate, Legal)

**Success Metrics:**
- Conversion rate: 3.5% → 4.5%
- Demo plays per visit: 8% → 12%
- Time on page: +30%

---

### 2. **SEO & Content Strategy** (High)
**Owner:** CMO → Copywriter & Social Media Expert
**Timeline:** Q1-Q2 2026

**Objectives:**
- Rank top 3 for 10 high-intent keywords
- Generate 5,000 organic monthly visitors by Q2 end
- Build topical authority in AI voice automation

**Key Initiatives:**
- Publish 2 pillar pages per vertical (Healthcare, Real Estate, etc.)
- Launch blog with 2 articles/week (use cases, ROI calculators, industry trends)
- Create comparison pages (Callengo vs. competitors, vs. manual calling, vs. BPOs)
- Build resource hub (templates, guides, ROI calculator)
- Implement structured data (FAQ schema, Organization schema, Product schema)
- Build backlink strategy (guest posts, partnerships, case studies)

**Target Keywords:**
- "AI phone agent for healthcare"
- "automated appointment confirmation"
- "lead qualification automation"
- "AI voice agent for real estate"
- "data validation service"

**Success Metrics:**
- Organic traffic: 500 → 5,000/mo
- Domain authority: 15 → 30
- Backlinks: 20 → 150

**Owner:** CTO
**Timeline:** Q1 2026

**Success Metrics:**

- LCP: <2.5s
- FID: <100ms
- CLS: <0.1

## Risk Mitigation

**Risk:** CTO and CMO capacity constraints before team expansion
**Mitigation:** Prioritize highest-ROI initiatives, defer integration showcase to Q2, use contractors for design assets if needed

**Risk:** SEO timeline (content takes 3-6 months to rank)
**Mitigation:** Start immediately, focus on low-competition long-tail keywords first, combine with paid acquisition strategy (owned by main Callengo app team)

**Risk:** Technical debt in legacy code affecting velocity
**Mitigation:** Allocate 10% CTO time to refactoring, document decisions in ADRs, maintain test coverage
